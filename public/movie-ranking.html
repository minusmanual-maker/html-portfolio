Top 3 Movies According To Shreya

My top 3 movies of all time

Extreme Job

Korean action-comedy movie. About a group of detectives trying to bring down a drug trade. Hilarious, great action scenes and good story. Anytime pick-me-up movie

Warning: will give an intense craving for fried chicken.

Sarusuberi (Ms. Hokusai)

Japanese slice-of-life anime movie. About the daughter of the famous Edo period painter Hokusai. Gentle, touching movie with some great dialogues and incredible art style and animation.

Morning Glory

American comedy drama. About Rachel McAdams working as a Morning news show producer with news anchor Harrison Ford. Very feel-good, beautiful soundtrack and gorgeous visuals.
